The Best Pierogi Recipe


Ingredients

  • Salt & Pepper
  • Cheese
  • Flour
  • Potatoes
  • Onion
  • Butter
  • Eggs

Cooking Instructions

  1. Make the Dough
  2. Make the Filling
  3. Mash and combine with shredded cheddar cheese
  4. Roll dough into 1/8" thick and 3" in diameter
  5. Fill each circle with the mashed potato filling and press edges to seal
